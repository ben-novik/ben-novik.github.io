Math Tutoring for Elementary & Middle School Students

Hi, I'm Ben.

I’m a math tutor who works with younger students, helping them build a strong foundation and actually understand what they’re learning — not just memorize steps.

Why I Work Well With Kids

I’ve spent years helping and taking care of my little brother and younger cousins, and I’ve worked with children at summer camp. Through all of that, I’ve learned how important patience is. Kids learn best when they feel comfortable, supported, and able to ask questions without pressure.

My approach is calm, step-by-step, and focused on building confidence. I make math feel simpler and more logical so kids actually start to feel good about it.

What I Tutor

  • Basic math skills (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division)
  • Fractions & decimals
  • Word problems and logical thinking
  • Pre-Algebra
  • Algebra I
  • Homework help + test preparation

How Sessions Work

I offer one-on-one, in-person math tutoring and charge by the hour. The first session is mainly about understanding how your child learns and identifying any areas where they feel stuck.

We’ll look over homework, quizzes, and tests so I can understand what’s confusing or intimidating. Every child learns differently, so I adapt my teaching style to match what works best for them.
